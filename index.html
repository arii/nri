National Robotics Initiative PI Meeting 2017

November 9th and 10th, 2017

The 2017 National Robotics Initiative (NRI) PI Meeting will be held on November 9th and 10th in Washington, DC.

The event will bring together the community of researchers, companies, and program managers who are actively engaged in the NRI program to share results, discuss findings, and learn about the latest topics in robotics research, commercialization, education, and outreach.

The agenda includes plenary sessions on emerging trends in robotics research, the start-up culture and industry keynotes.

Program

The NRI meeting features a two day program that includes poster sessions, rapid fire summaries from all the projects, several plenary sessions, demos, and ample social time. The schedule will be updated below.

RAPID TALKS

Two minute talks will be presented from your own laptops. No electronic submission of the presentation is necessary. If you have embedded video or are unsure if your laptop will connect with the hotel presentation system (which should have HDMI, VGA, minidisplayport) please check before your session during one of the breaks.

Odd numbered presentations will present on the podium stage left. Even numbered presentations on the podium stage right. Just before your session, please locate yourselves near the front so you may quickly setup in the 2 minutes you have as we alternate left and right

POSTERS

The electronic submission of the posters should be to our dropbox locker anytime before the meeting. This is only for archival purposes.

Bring your hard copy poster to pin up on our 4×4’ poster board. First day presenters please pin-up your poster anytime before your poster session on the first day. Posters need to be taken down at the end of the day. Second day presenters please pin-up your posters any time before your session on the second day. The same poster stands will be used for both days.
